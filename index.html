Welcome to the Virtual Rube Goldberg Machine!

  • Click on the dropdown to add objects to the playground. New objects will appear in the sandbox.
  • Drag and drop objects onto the grass.
  • To rotate, press the 'r' key.
    To translate, press the 't' key.
    To scale, press the 's' key.
    Then, use your mouse to apply the transformation!
  • To start your RGM, include an angled domino or a ball over an inclined plane. Then click start.
  • To clear the playground, press restart on the dropdown. Then begin building a new RGM!
